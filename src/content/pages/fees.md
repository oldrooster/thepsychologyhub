title: "Fees"
---

Each clinician at The Psychology Hub sets their own fees. We encourage you to contact your chosen clinician directly.

## General Information

Psychological therapy sessions are typically 50-60 minutes in length. Fees range from approximately $200-$280 per session.

## ACC Funding

Several clinicians are ACC registered providers. If your difficulties are related to a personal injury, you may be eligible for subsidised sessions.

## Health Insurance

Many health insurance policies cover psychological therapy. We can provide invoices in the required format.

## Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may be charged at the full session rate.
