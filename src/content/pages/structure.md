The Psychology Hub is a group practice located in Riccarton, Christchurch. We bring together a diverse team of clinicians including clinical psychologists, psychologists, nurse therapists, social workers, and dietitians.

## How We Work

Our clinicians are independent practitioners who share a professional home at The Psychology Hub. Each clinician manages their own referrals, fees, and appointments, while benefiting from the collegial environment and shared administration support the Hub provides.

## Referrals

Referrals can be made directly to individual clinicians. You are welcome to contact us to discuss which clinician may be the best fit for your needs.

## Location

We are based at Suite 3, 21 Leslie Hills Drive, Riccarton, Christchurch.

## Professional Development

We are committed to ongoing professional development and evidence-based practice.
